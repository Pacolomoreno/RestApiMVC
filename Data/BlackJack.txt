                                      Blackjack Rules – The Basics
_______________________________________________________________________________________________________________

    Blackjack (also called 21) is a popular casino card game where players compete against the dealer,
     not each other. The goal is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer’s hand without 
     going over (busting).

1. Card Values

        (Ace) = 1 or 11     (2-10) = value      (J, Q, K) = 10 

2. How to Play

    Dealer deals a card to the player sum values and decide betwen hit a new card or stand 
    in closest value to 21 without busting over.

    Dealer reveals his hand value and hands are compared—whoever is closer to 21 
    without busting wins and get reward while other lose it.

3. Player Options

    Hit → Take another card.
    Stand → Keep your current hand.

4. After each play you have the chance to :

    p : Continue playing
    d : Double bet
    s : Split bet to the half
    e : End game .. Take your money and run !!!
